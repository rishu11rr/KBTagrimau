About KBT Agri Research Producer Co. Ltd.

KBT Agri Research Producer Co. Ltd., based in Mau, is a leading agricultural company dedicated to enhancing farming productivity through high-quality products. Founded by Mr. Deendayal Kushwaha, the company offers a comprehensive range of agricultural inputs, including premium seeds, advanced fertilizers, and effective crop medicines. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, KBT Agri Research aims to support farmers in achieving higher yields and healthier crops, contributing to the overall growth of the agricultural sector.
